# DermaAI User Guide

Welcome to **DermaAI**, your AI-powered dermatological assistant. This guide will walk you through the core features and workflows for both patients and healthcare professionals.

> [!CAUTION]
> **MEDICAL DISCLAIMER**
> DermaAI is a Proof of Concept (POC) designed for educational and informational purposes only. It uses Artificial Intelligence to provide preliminary analysis of skin conditions. **It is NOT a diagnostic tool.** Users must always consult a qualified medical professional for diagnosis and treatment.

## üë§ Patient Journey

### 1. Getting Started
- **Landing Page**: Start at the homepage to learn about DermaAI's services.
- **Anonymous Trial**: Use the **"Try without signing up"** option on the landing page for a quick AI assessment. Note that these cases are not saved.
- **Registration/Login**: Create an account or log in to save your history and connect with a doctor.

### 2. Connecting with a Doctor
- **Select a Doctor**: On your first visit to the **Patient Dashboard**, you will be prompted to select a dermatologist from our list of available professionals.
- **Change Doctor**: You can change your linked doctor at any time from the dashboard, provided you don't have an active case pending review.

### 3. Uploading and Analysis
- **New Scan**: Click **"New Scan"** or **"Analyze"** to start.
- **Upload Image**: Select a clear, well-lit photo of the affected area (JPG, PNG, or WebP).
- **AI Analysis**: The system will generate a preliminary report including potential conditions, confidence levels, and general recommendations.
- **AI Chat**: Use the chat panel below the results to ask follow-up questions like *"What does this mean?"* or *"Should I be worried?"*.

### 4. Physician Review
- **Request Review**: If you want a human expert to look at your case, click **"Request Physician Review"**. 
- **Wait for Triage**: Your case status will move to **"Pending"**. Once a doctor joins, the AI chat will be paused, and you can communicate directly with your physician.

### 5. Managing History
- **View History**: Access all your past scans and reports from the **"View History"** button on your dashboard.

### 1. Accessing the Dashboard
- **Login**: Log in using your professional credentials (e.g., `alice@derma.com` / `password123`).
- **Dashboard Overview**: See a list of your linked patients and any active cases requiring attention.

### 2. Case Management
- **Review Cases**: Click on a patient to see their full profile or select a specific case from the dashboard.
- **Triage**: View the high-resolution images uploaded by the patient alongside the AI's preliminary findings.
- **Doctor Chat**: Join the conversation to provide professional advice, ask follow-up questions, or provide a clinical diagnosis.
