## üî¨ AI-Powered Analysis

### How DermaAI Works

1. **Patient Upload:** Patients upload clear images of skin concerns via the DermaAI platform
2. **Preliminary AI Analysis:** Google Gemini API provides instant, educational analysis
3. **Doctor Review:** Licensed dermatologist reviews analysis and clinical history
4. **Professional Diagnosis:** Doctor provides medically authoritative diagnosis and treatment plan
5. **Follow-Up Care:** Patients track progress through integrated dashboard and messaging

### Technology Stack
- **AI Engine:** Google Gemini API for image analysis
- **Platform:** Cloud-hosted, HIPAA-aligned architecture (POC)
- **Availability:** 24/7 upload; doctor response times vary by clinic

## ‚öïÔ∏è Medical Safety & Disclaimer

### ‚ö†Ô∏è **CRITICAL MEDICAL DISCLAIMER**

**This application is a Proof of Concept (POC) for educational and informational purposes ONLY.**

- **DermaAI is NOT a diagnostic tool.** It provides preliminary, AI-generated educational analysis only.
- **Consult a qualified medical professional** for any diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice.
- **The AI analysis is supplementary.** It assists doctors but does not replace clinical judgment.
- **Emergency cases:** If you suspect a skin malignancy or have severe symptoms, seek in-person emergency care immediately.

### Patient Responsibilities
- Provide clear, high-quality images for accurate analysis
- Disclose complete medical history and current medications
- Follow-up with assigned doctor for professional diagnosis
- Seek emergency care for urgent conditions

### Doctor Responsibilities
- Review all cases with full clinical skepticism
- Request additional tests/imaging if AI analysis is inconclusive
- Provide written, signed documentation for legal compliance
- Escalate uncertain cases to specialist colleagues
- Never provide diagnosis based solely on AI output

### Data Privacy
- All patient data is encrypted and stored securely
- HIPAA compliance measures are in place (POC stage)
- Patients control data sharing permissions
- Images are processed and retained according to clinic policy
